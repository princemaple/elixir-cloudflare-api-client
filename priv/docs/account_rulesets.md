# Account rulesets

Account-level rulesets

## List account rulesets

`GET` List all rulesets for an account

> GET accounts/:account_id/rulesets

**Permission needed:** `None`

Available in:

* FREE
* PRO
* BUSINESS
* ENTERPRISE
